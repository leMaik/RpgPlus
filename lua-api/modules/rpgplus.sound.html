Module rpgplus.sound

Provides sound and music functions.

Functions

playSound (player, sound) Play a sound to a specific player.
playNote (player, instrument, note) Play a note to a specific player.

Functions

playSound (player, sound)
Play a sound to a specific player.

Parameters:

  • player player to play the sound to
  • sound sound to play
playNote (player, instrument, note)
Play a note to a specific player.

Parameters:

  • player player to play the note to
  • instrument instrument which will playing
  • note note to play, either its number of a string like "c#'" or "a"
playSong (player, song)
Play a song to a specific player.

Parameters:

  • player player to play the song to
  • song path of the .nbs song file, relative to the script directory

Returns:

    true if the song was started, false if not (due to missing NoteBlockAPI plugin)
